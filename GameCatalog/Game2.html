Miitopia

In Miitopia, you'll take on the role of a hero who must save the world from the Dark Lord, who has been stealing faces from the inhabitants and placing them on monsters. Choose from various job classes like warrior, mage, cleric, and more to build your perfect team. As you progress through the story, you'll strengthen relationships between party members, leading to special bonuses in battle. The game features a unique relationship system where Mii characters can become friends, develop chemistry, and even get into arguments that affect their performance in battle. You can customize not only your own character but also create Mii versions of friends, family, or anyone you want to populate the entire game world. With colorful graphics, humorous dialogue, and engaging RPG mechanics, Miitopia offers a delightful mix of social simulation and adventure that's suitable for players of all ages. The game includes features like an inn where your party members can rest, bond, and engage in silly activities, as well as various mini-games and side quests to discover.
